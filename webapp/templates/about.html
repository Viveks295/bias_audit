About this project

AI Bias Audit is a lightweight web frontend for the bias_audit Python package. Upload a CSV of prompts and essays, supply your grading function, pick linguistic variations (spelling errors, pronoun swaps, etc.), and the tool quantifies how much each variation shifts the model's score — highlighting potential biases in automated essay-grading systems.
